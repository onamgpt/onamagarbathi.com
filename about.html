Our Story

A century of sacred fragrance — from the workshops of Bangalore to the world

Home › About Us

"Continuing the fragrance legacy of National Flag Perfumery Works, founded 1922 — over a century of devotion, craftsmanship, and aromatic excellence, from our family to yours."

The story of Onam Agarbathi begins in 1922, when our great-grandfather established National Flag Perfumery Works in Bangalore. What started as a small workshop crafting incense with traditional hand-rolled techniques has grown into one of India's most recognised agarbathi brands — trusted in homes, temples, and puja rooms across the country and around the globe.

In 1972, the Onam Agarbathi brand was formally launched, marking 50 years of our family's fragrance heritage. The brand brought with it a new commitment to quality, packaging, and reach — and quickly established itself as a national name. Our flagship product, Black Gem, became the incense that serious enthusiasts ask for by name.

Over the following decades, we expanded our portfolio to 30 distinct fragrances spanning premium masala, devotional, aromatherapy, multi-faith, sacred resin, and collector's editions — each crafted with the same devotion and precision that has defined our family's work for generations.

Our Export Legacy

Onam Agarbathi has a long and proud history of international trade. In earlier decades, we exported to more than 35 countries — and while some of those trading relationships have evolved over time, we remain actively engaged in export to Trinidad & Tobago, Dubai, Muscat, Surinam, and Guyana, serving the Indian diaspora in each of these markets.

We are now actively building fresh export relationships, with a focus on markets wherever incense is integral to daily life — Southeast Asia, the Middle East, the Caribbean, and the Indian diaspora communities in Malaysia, Singapore, the UK, and North America.

Looking Ahead

The next chapter of Onam Agarbathi is being written with ambition: premium brands suited for India's quick commerce platforms, e-commerce channels for the global diaspora, and future innovations in fragrance technology including AI-assisted perfume development. Our 100-year foundation gives us the confidence to build boldly for the next century.

We are also exploring government contracts and institutional supply expansion, alongside a dedicated sales presence in Kerala and direct marketing channels across South India.
