A Heritage of Sacred Fragrance

Founded in 1922 by our great-grandfather under the name National Flag Perfumery Works, Onam Agarbathi Pvt. Ltd. has carried forward a century of aromatic excellence — from the workshops of Bangalore to puja rooms, homes, and hearts across India and the world.

Registered as a proud MSME with a turnover of approximately ₹10 Crore, we supply India's CSD/Defence Canteens, hold central government contracts of 40+ years, and export to destinations including Trinidad & Tobago, Dubai, Muscat, Surinam, and Guyana.

Our Full Story
1922 Founded as National Flag Perfumery Works by our great-grandfather in Bangalore.
1972 Onam Agarbathi brand launched — establishing a new chapter of fragrance excellence.
1980s Secured Central Government contracts; began supplying CSD Defence Canteens across India.
Today 30 fragrances, 35+ export countries, award-winning packaging, and expanding globally.
